Zihao Zhao

I'm a Master's student in the Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen Institution (TBSI) at Tsinghua University, fortunately advised by Wenbo Ding. I previously interned at the Bing News & Feeds Group in Microsoft, the Social Computing Group in MSRA, and the Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR) in Tsinghua University.

My research interests lie primarily in distributed learning, optimization, and machine learning. Currently, I mainly study the following topics: federated learning, communication efficiency, and their generalization analysis.
